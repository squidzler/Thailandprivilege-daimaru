Programs · Bronze

Bronze — The simplest entry, five years.

Bronze is the most-applied Thailand Privilege tier: five years of long-stay, multi-entry residency on a single application, with every core service included. Annual Privilege Points begin from Gold.

Start your application Download forms
At a glance

Bronze, in numbers.

  • One-time membership fee ฿650,000 (~$19,000 USD)
  • Term 5 years
  • Annual Privilege Points No annual points — all core services included
  • Family Individual membership (no family add-ons)
  • Application Open · application window to 30 September 2026

Thai baht is the price of record; the USD figure is approximate. The fee is paid once, to Thailand Privilege Card Co., Ltd., only after approval — never to Daimaru, and never upfront. Compare all tiers on the programs page.

What's included

Core services, every tier.

  • Airport fast track and meet-and-greet on arrival and departure.
  • A 24-hour member contact centre — one point of help for the practical questions of settling in.
  • Help with the in-passport stay stamp for members living in Thailand.
  • Privilege Points — annual points begin from Gold; Bronze includes all core services.

The full benefit list is on the benefits page. Best for: Solo applicants who want long-term Thai residency without committing to decades.
