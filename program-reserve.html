Reserve — Twenty years. By invitation.

Reserve is the longest-duration tier: twenty years of residency, the deepest annual points allocation, and family inclusion. Membership is by invitation only.

At a glance

Reserve, in numbers.

  • One-time membership fee ฿5,000,000 (~$146,000 USD)
  • Term 20 years
  • Annual Privilege Points 120 Privilege Points per year
  • Family Family add-ons available (+฿2,000,000 per member)
  • Application By invitation only

Thai baht is the price of record; the USD figure is approximate. The fee is paid once, to Thailand Privilege Card Co., Ltd., only after approval — never to Daimaru, and never upfront. Compare all tiers on the programs page.

What's included

Core services, every tier.

  • Airport fast track and meet-and-greet on arrival and departure.
  • A 24-hour member contact centre — one point of help for the practical questions of settling in.
  • Help with the in-passport stay stamp for members living in Thailand.
  • Privilege Points — 120 privilege points per year, redeemable for transfers, spa, golf, dining and more.

The full benefit list is on the benefits page. Best for: Those making Thailand a permanent base who want the longest horizon and the fullest benefits.
