Who we are

The license behind the service.

Daimaru Trading Co., Ltd. is a Japan-registered company, headquartered in Sendai, Miyagi, authorized by Thailand Privilege Card Co., Ltd. — the state-affiliated operator of the Thailand Privilege Visa program — to receive, prepare, and submit applications on the operator's behalf. That authorization, granted under license number SA15/029, has been continuously in force since 2015.

Our tenure spans the program's rebrand from Thailand Elite Visa to Thailand Privilege Visa in late 2023 — a point at which many newer agents entered the market and several older ones did not continue. Our relationship with the program operator predates the current brand.

The information on this page is intended to give a prospective applicant the means to verify our authority, longevity, and ongoing activity before deciding whom to engage.

Scope of engagement

What the agent does — and does not.

A prospective applicant unfamiliar with the model often assumes the agent is a salesperson. The work is more substantial than that. For a typical applicant, our engagement covers:

  • Pre-qualification review. We assess eligibility, nationality-specific considerations, and family-eligibility constraints before any application is submitted. We decline to submit applications that are unlikely to be approved.
  • Document preparation. Passport bio page, photograph, signed application form, and tier-specific supporting documents — all reviewed for completeness and consistency before submission.
  • Submission and liaison. Applications are submitted to Thailand Privilege Card Co., Ltd., which then conducts a background check across five Thai government agencies. The process typically takes four to eight weeks, and we serve as your principal point of contact throughout.
  • Payment coordination. The one-time membership fee is paid only after the application has been approved, directly to the program operator. We do not collect the fee in advance and do not benefit from unapproved applications.
  • Visa affixation and onboarding. Coordination with the program operator and Thai Immigration for visa affixation, member-benefit setup, and initial entry support.
  • Ongoing support during your tenure. Including the in-passport stay-stamp renewal for members residing in Thailand continuously for more than one year per entry. The membership is paid once and covers the full 5- to 20-year duration — there are no annual membership fees. The one-year stay stamp is a separate, immigration-administered matter applicable only to continuous residents.

We do not provide tax, immigration, or legal advice. Members with tax-residency or legal questions are referred to qualified specialists.
