(“Daimaru,” “we,” “us”), an authorized General Sales & Services Agent (License No. SA15/029) for Thailand Privilege Card Co., Ltd. For any privacy question or request, contact us at info@thailandelite.net. Registered office: [PLACEHOLDER — company registered address].

Information we collect

  • Information you give us. When you submit an enquiry or begin an application, we collect details such as your name, email address, phone number, nationality, the membership tier you are interested in, and any message or information you provide. If you proceed with an application, we collect the further details needed to prepare and submit it.
  • Information collected automatically. Our hosting provider records standard technical data (such as IP address and request logs) for security and reliability. This site stores small preferences in your browser (for example, your chosen display currency); these are functional and are not used for advertising.
  • We do not currently run third-party advertising or analytics cookies on this site. If that changes, this policy will be updated and, where required, your consent requested.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide the information or consultation you ask for.
  • To prepare, submit, and support your Thailand Privilege Visa application with Thailand Privilege Card Co., Ltd. and the relevant Thai authorities.
  • To keep records, meet legal obligations, and protect against misuse.

For visitors in the EU/UK, our legal bases are your consent, the taking of steps at your request before entering a contract and performing it, and our legitimate interest in responding to enquiries.

How we share your information

  • Thailand Privilege Card Co., Ltd. — the program operator, to process and approve your application.
  • Shared contact infrastructure. Enquiries to this site are handled by Daimaru Trading and may be received and managed through a shared inbox and contact channels also used by our sister website, www.thailandelite.net (operated by the same company and team), so that we can respond from one place.
  • Service providers that help us operate — for example our website host and the messaging channels you choose to use (WhatsApp, LINE, email).
  • Authorities, where disclosure is required by law.
  • We do not sell your personal information.

International transfers

We operate from Thailand, and your information may be processed in Thailand and in other countries where our service providers operate. Where required, we take steps to ensure your information remains protected when it is transferred.

How long we keep it

We keep your information only as long as needed to assist you and to meet our legal and record-keeping obligations, after which it is deleted or anonymised. [PLACEHOLDER — state a specific retention period if you wish, e.g. “enquiry records are kept for 24 months”.]

Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your information, to object to or restrict certain processing, and to withdraw consent. Residents of Thailand are protected under the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA); residents of the EU/UK under the GDPR. To exercise any right, email info@thailandelite.net and we will respond within the time the law allows.

Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to keep your data safe and limit access to those who need it.

Children

This site is intended for adults considering long-stay residency. We do not knowingly collect information from children except as part of a family application made by a responsible adult.
